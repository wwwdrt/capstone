Featured Speakers

Sugar Ray

Retired Professional Boxer and Boxing Analyst

Sugar Ray Leonard is a retired professional boxer and Olympic gold medalist. He is considered one of the greatest boxers of all time and was inducted into the International Boxing Hall of Fame in 1997. He is now a boxing analyst for various sports networks. Leonard attended Palmer Park High School and the University of Maryland.

Bob Arum

Boxing Promoter

Bob Arum is a boxing promoter and the founder and CEO of Top Rank Boxing, one of the most successful boxing promotion companies in the world. He has promoted some of the biggest boxing events in history and has worked with legendary fighters such as Muhammad Ali, Manny Pacquiao, and Floyd Mayweather Jr. Arum graduated from Harvard Law School

Eddie Hearn

Boxing Promoter

Eddie Hearn is a boxing promoter and the managing director of Matchroom Sport, a leading sports promotion company that specializes in boxing. He has promoted some of the biggest boxing events in recent years and has worked with high-profile boxers such as Anthony Joshua, Kell Brook, and Amir Khan. Hearn attended the University of Essex.

Dr. Margaret Goodman

Medical Doctor and Boxing Safety Advocate

Dr. Margaret Goodman is a medical doctor and the founder and president of the Voluntary Anti-Doping Association (VADA). She is a leading advocate for the safety of boxers and has worked to implement medical and safety protocols in the sport. Dr. Goodman received her medical degree from the University of Nevada School of Medicine.

Bernard Hopkins

Retired Professional Boxer and Boxing Analyst

Bernard Hopkins is a retired professional boxer and former world champion in two weight classes. He is considered one of the greatest middleweight boxers of all time and was inducted into the International Boxing Hall of Fame in 2020. He is now a boxing analyst for various sports networks. Hopkins attended Graterford State Correctional Institution, where he earned his GED.

Mauricio Sulaiman

Boxing Administrator and Advocate

Mauricio Sulaiman is the president of the World Boxing Council (WBC), one of the four major professional boxing organizations in the world. He has worked to implement safety and medical protocols in the sport and has been an advocate for the rights of boxers. Sulaiman attended the Instituto Tecnologico y de Estudios Superiores de Monterrey in Mexico.
