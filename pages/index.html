WORLD BOXING
CONFERENCE
2023

Welcome to the World Boxing Conference where champions are born and legacies are made. Join a global community of fighters and enthusiasts alike, and experience the electrifying rush of the sport that's captured hearts and minds around the world. Discover the power of the punch, the discipline of the training, and the camaraderie of the ring. Whether you're a seasoned pro or just starting out, the WBC is the ultimate destination for anyone looking to elevate their game and leave their mark on the world of boxing. Join us today and become part of the legacy.

2023.10.15(THU) ~ 16(FRI)

@ Sheikh Zayed Rd, Dubai World Trade Centre and more

Main Program

Champion's Mindset

Get inside the head of the world's top boxing champions and learn their winning strategies and mental toughness. Join us for this powerful lecture and gain insights to help you conquer your own battles.

Boxing Revolution

Discover the latest trends and innovations in the world of boxing. This exhibition will showcase cutting-edge technology and equipment, as well as revolutionary training techniques. Be part of the future of boxing!

Ringside Forum

Join the discussion with the industry's top experts and influencers. This interactive forum will explore the current state of boxing, its challenges and opportunities, and the road ahead. Don't miss this unique opportunity to voice your opinion and shape the future of the sport.

Power Punch Workshop

Unleash your full potential in the ring with this hands-on workshop. Learn the proper technique for delivering a knockout punch, and master footwork, speed, and agility. Led by world-class trainers, this workshop will take your boxing skills to the next level.

Ignite the Fight

Get inspired by the incredible stories of resilience and determination from boxers who have overcome adversity. This motivational ignite session will feature powerful talks from athletes and coaches who have conquered the odds and achieved greatness. Fuel your own fighting spirit and join us for an unforgettable experience.
